A few words from the founder

Nowadays AI is present almost everywhere. What you're seeing here was made to help bridge the distance between the idea of the blackbox AI represented and the possibility of learning about it in a hands on way - for you as much as it was for me while building it.

After seven months of dedicated work and with the publication of this website, my passion project enters the last stage of production - your experience as a user. It is a huge gain for me to hear about your experience here, so please feel free to contact me using the information given on this website. Let's improve Joi together!

Every step of Joi's development has been shared openly and transparently online, from the early concept sketches to training experiments and live demos.
